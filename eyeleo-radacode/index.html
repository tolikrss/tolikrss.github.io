An app that keeps your eyes healthy.

Eye Leo for Mac is an app that regulary reminds you to take a rest for your eyes, shows you simple eye exercises and prevents you from using Mac during these exercises. When you're working at a computer it's easy to forget about about time. This leads to eye strain and other physical problems. EyeLeo guarantees you won't forget to take a break.

Features:

Screen blocking during break exercises.

Short break with eye exercises.

Visual explanation of every exercises.

Notifications announcing a long break coming.

Strict mode that doesn't allow skipping the breaks

Customizable preferences.
